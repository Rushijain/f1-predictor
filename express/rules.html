Rules and Regulations

  • Qualifying Prediction Panel will be locked 30 minutes before Q1 starts
  • Race Prediction Panel will be locked 30 minutes before the lights out
  • Organizers are not liable for delayed predictions. Please ensure you submit your predictions on time.
  • Pick at least one driver per race.
  • You must pick 3 unique drivers for the race result.
  • Fastest Lap, Pole position and Winning Teams predictions are optional.
  • Streak points are given at the end of the streak, after which the streak resets.
  • The Pre-Season Predictions closes on 26th March
  • The Mid-Season Predictions closes on 25th August
  • The Pre-Season and Mid-Season Predictions are optional (No negative points). Bonus points are available for Pre-Season and Mid-Season Predictions
  • Read up on resources on the Info Sheet. Join the community here
