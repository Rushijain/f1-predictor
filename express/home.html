Rules and Regulations

  • Qualifying Prediction Panel will be locked 30 minutes before qualifying 1 starts for Pole position predictions
  • Race Prediction Panel will be locked 30 minutes before the Race begins for all other predictions
  • IT IS YOUR RESPONSIBILITY TO ENTER PREDICTIONS ON TIME. PLEASE PLAN AHEAD.
  • You must pick AT LEAST ONE DRIVER per race to ensure you don't lose points.
  • You must pick 3 UNIQUE DRIVERS for the race result
  • Fastest Lap, Pole position and Winning Constructor per race are optional predictions. You do not lose points if you don't predict here
  • Streak points are given at the end of the streak, after which the streak resets
  • The PRE-SEASON Predictions have to be completed BY 24th March
  • The MID-SEASON Predictions have to be completed BY 25th August
  • The PRE-SEASON and MID-SEASON Predictions are OPTIONAL. You do not lose points if you don't predict here
  • You can get points for BOTH PRE-SEASON and MID-SEASON Predictions
  • If you're new to F1, do read the "INFO SHEET" where you'll find some good resources to get started. Join Whatsapp Group from here
